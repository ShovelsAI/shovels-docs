**The Shovels free trial includes full permit and contractor search, access to the last 12 months of data, all filters, and API quota for 250 records.** No credit card required. Paid plans unlock CSV downloads, 25+ years of historical data, and higher API quota.

## Features Available in the Free Trial

### Permit & Contractor Search

Users can access the **permit** and **contractor** search functionality from the left side of the user interface.

### Data Availability

The free trial includes access to the last 12 months of permit and contractor data. Paying customers get access to all historical Shovels data (on average, 25+ years).

### Filters and Sorting

Users can utilize various filters to refine their searches. Filters are available for categories such as:
- Financial
- Building
- Permit
- Contractor

Paying customers can download search results and sort them in Excel or Google Sheets.

### Data Refresh Rate

Shovels data is updated monthly. See [data refresh frequency](/docs/knowledge-base/data/quality/refresh-frequency) for details.

## Getting Started

1. [Create a free account](https://app.shovels.ai) to begin your trial
2. Use the search functionality to explore permits and contractors
3. Apply filters to narrow down your results
4. Explore contractor profiles and permit details
